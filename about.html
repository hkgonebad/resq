Reliance resQ is India's first ISO:9001 certified, dedicated after-sales service brand offering multi-product, multi-brand electronics services. Our dedicated team of over 2000 experts across 175 cities are committed to delivering the highest quality care and a personalised experience for our customers.

Extended Warranty:
Our unique extended warranty programme, resQ Care goes one step further than brand warranty to ensure a hassle-free experience for years to come. resQ Care covers the servicing and replacement of all functional parts, most electrical and mechanical failures and even offers an 80% buyback policy in case of irreparable damage.
